# Acme Widgets Platform

## Overview
A small SaaS for managing widget inventory. Users sign in through the frontend, CRUD their widgets via the backend API, and receive periodic email digests generated by a background worker.

## External Systems
- **Postgres**: Primary data store. Shared by backend and worker.
- **SendGrid**: Transactional email delivery. Called by the worker.
- **Auth0**: Authentication provider. Integrated with the frontend.

## Actors
- **End user**: Signs in, manages their widget inventory through the frontend.
- **Ops engineer**: Monitors the worker queue and deployment health.
